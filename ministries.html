Church Ministries

Men's Ministry

The Men's Ministry at New House of Grace International is dedicated to equipping and empowering men to grow in faith, leadership, and service. Through fellowship, prayer, and mentorship, we encourage men to lead godly lives, strengthen their families, and impact their communities with Christ-centered values.

Women's Ministry

The Women's Ministry at New House of Grace International is a vibrant community where women grow spiritually, support one another, and serve with love. Through prayer, mentorship, and fellowship, we empower women to live out their faith, strengthen their families, and make a positive impact in their communities.

Children's Ministry

The Children's Ministry provides a safe and joyful environment for kids to learn about God's love through fun, interactive, and age-appropriate biblical lessons. Our goal is to lay a strong spiritual foundation, helping children grow in faith and develop Christ-like character from an early age.

Youth Ministry

The Youth Ministry is a dynamic space where young people are nurtured in their faith and equipped to navigate life with biblical principles. Through engaging teachings, mentorship, and community outreach, we inspire the next generation to live with purpose and make a difference for Christ.
