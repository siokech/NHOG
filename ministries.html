Our Church Ministries

Everyone Gets a Place to Serve at New House of Grace International. Come expecting to receive grace to serve the living God.

Women's Ministry


We seek to empower the women in a manner that teaches them to follow in God-given biblical place. That wife, mother, daughter, and member of society are responsible in all their ways. With decency and responsible bringing glory to God. In essence, the NHoGI Ministries are keen to create awareness among the ladies on matters of self-esteem, economic empowerment, and social responsibility counselling with the purpose to drive social-economic growth in their societies. The ministry is also charged with training and preparing ladies for various roles in leadership and mentorship for other girls, they are tasked with championing moral correction among the girls both in and out through community sensitizations, training, and outreaches. This has been one of the areas we have registered success to the glory of God. The women also seek to foster oneness among the ladies by lending a hand to carry another sister in trouble and encouraging those that are in a bad place. We seek to see women take up their places and roles in homes and society in general.

Praise & Worship Ministry


The Voice of Triumph (VoT) leads powerful praise and worship sessions every week at NHoGI being a very important aspect of any ministry, our unique delivery and the ministration are distinct. We seek to edify the spirit of man to connect with the maker in song and dance. The team comprises various ethnicities and cultures from all across Africa & beyond and we do it with a passion for the glory of God. Our NHoGI Worship Ministry also offers training mentorship programs for the children’s church on vocals, instruments, etc

Men's Ministry


As the years have gone by and modernization has taken center stage with the message of women’s emancipation, it’s no surprise that in so many ways men have been sidelined and left to play second fiddle in relationships, society, and government.

The Men of Valor at NHoGI Ministries seek to teach and admonish fellow men in God-given leadership roles. The ministry’s core mandates are:

  • -To teach men to fulfill their God-given mandate as heads, leaders, and providers.
  • -To teach men to be fathers both at home and on missions, in a generation where fathers have left their place for one reason or another.
  • -To teach men to be the symbols of unity and love both at home and away, to be responsible and accountable.
  • -To encourage men to maintain a biblical standard in their approach to the steadfastly changing customs today.
  • -To offer counseling and related assistance to men, young and old, in their personal lives or walk with Christ.
  • -To create a unity platform for men to share ideas and unwind.

With the patronage of Apostle Ken, the men have embraced their duties and actively serve in different departments, from ushering to protocol, worship, and children’s church, all to the glory of God.

Married/Family Ministry


The heart of any civilization is family. today it’s under threat and as a ministry, we are passionate to see thriving family units as it’s the bedrock of our nation. we seek to restore the sanctity of marriage and we have witnessed a growing number of young people freely entering it. We believe that God instituted marriage to be between one man and one woman. At NHoGI the married ministry offers premarital counselling to new couples and offers couples therapy to couples that need it. it’s also a conflict resolution docket for all matters pertaining to marriage, and family. Apostle Ken and Rev. Judy Baraza are the chief patrons with the head of marrieds serving directly under them.

Youth Ministry


We are dynamic young adults with a passion for Christ.

As the youth arm of the ministry, equipped to set the world aflame for Christ, we are one of the fastest-growing and most vibrant youth churches in the world. We cherish grassroots growth through sound teachings, observance, listening, and responding to immediate needs. Our goal is to create a safe, loving, and wholesome atmosphere for young people to thrive.

Motto:

The Young, Brave, and Strong

Mission Statement:

To reach out to fellow youth with the zeal to transform society with the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

The Flame: Life-Giving Answers to Burning Questions

  • “What is my purpose?”
  • “Do I need God?”
  • “Now that I am saved?”

In response, the pastoral team delivers God’s revealed Word in an atmosphere that nurtures and strengthens the spiritual paths of our young society.
