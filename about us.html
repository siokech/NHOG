About Us

New House of Grace International: A Global Ministry of Impact

New House of Grace International (NHoGI) Ministries started in early 2015 as a house fellowship with Apostle Ken, his lovely wife Judy and a couple of brethren. Today, the ministry takes a tremendous stride in touching many lives globally. Launched officially in Johannesburg, South Africa, in 2016, the church now has a coverage of over 15 branches globally. Apostle has footprints across the globe through the mentorship of pastors, evangelists, and many sons and daughters trained and commissioned to continue the works of God across various countries. NHoGI was put to an establishment in obedience to the apostolic and prophetic call of God on the man of God, the Apostle, and wholly supported by the first lady Rev. Judy. The ministerial work can be traced from as far as 1996, when Apostle Ken responded to the call to minister and actively served in various capacities, being mentored by one of the best fathers this land has known. It’s his submission to spiritual authority, the fear of God, and a burning desire to see souls saved that set the course for life and a pattern of ministry that would later birth the New House of Grace Ministries, following many years of sacrifice and service. Known throughout Kenya as a model for harmony and diversity, the NHoGI congregation is multi-generational and multi-ethnic with members from within and beyond the borders. Each week, NHoGI reaches thousands of people in Kakamega Kenya, and across various locations globally, through worship services, powerful sermons, wisdom classes, online TV & radio, and local missions. NHoGI’s heart for serving makes a tangible impact on the surrounding community through its outreach programs for hospitals, prisons, the unchurched, door-to-door evangelism, schools and university missions, and other outreaches.

WHAT WE SEE

We see tremendous hunger in a people who seek the saving knowledge amidst very strong religious beliefs and surrounding sects, we see lives transformed and the moral fiber among the youth reshaping, family reunions, and spiritual nourishment. Such are the beats of joy that this ministry has brought to society to the glory of God. Kakamega, like many places, is not exactly an easy ground for the gospel, but by God’s grace we have been able to reach thousands with the message of Christ and we see the transformation and great deliverance in the lives of many.

THE VISION BEARERS

Apostle Ken Baraza

He is the founder and general overseer of New House of Grace International Ministry, and currently, is the senior pastor of NHoGI Kakamega, Kenya. The Ministries’ Head Quarters. His call to minister the Gospel came in the early 90s and he humbly and dedicatedly pursued it serving in all capacities from usher to, choir, to youth pastor during his early days. It was the pursuit of excellence with humility in the kingdom of God that built lasting principles and ushered him into elevated graces & anointing that makes him the man that he is today, an Apostle general of God! He has established several churches in East, Central, and South Africa and lectures in various bible colleges and schools around the world, he has also mentored many pastors, evangelists as well as sons and daughters serving actively in ministry. Maintaining a humble spirit and approachable demeanor, Ap. Ken, widely known as one of the firmest counselors acknowledges and attributes his life transformational process and ministry success to God. He is a widely sought-after public speaker, for conferences, seminars, and crusades with an uncommon insight into scriptures, as well as a quintessential author with several titles available in both print and audio. An eminent prophet, he ministers the revealed Word of God to many with precise utterances to the glory of God. His dedication to not only reveal Christ but also an anointed deliverance minister. He remains one who truly has a heart for souls and the welfare of people, he is dedicated to service in local governments as an advisor and has contributed immensely to some of the best policies serving the people. He is an ardent family man dyed-in-the-wool to the welfare of his lovely daughters, and his wife Rev. Judy Baraza.

Rev. Judy Baraza

She is a wife and co-founder of New House of Grace International, serving as a laborer with her husband, Apostle Ken. Rev. Judy is a pillar of strength to her husband and the ministry since its inception and continues to actively pursue her calling in ministerial preaching. She is the vision bearer and patron of the women’s ministry “DEBORAH ARISE” at the New House of Grace International Ministries. Her passion for the girl child, women, and family has made her one many turn to for advice and counseling. Reverent is a powerful worshiper in her own right and has been in active ministry for over 2 decades dating back to her school days, this hunger and fire to preach the gospel and mentor many has earned her the “Mother” title both at NHoGI and globally. As a result, it’s not strange the women’s ministry is one of the most thriving and the church has become a center of moral precision. She is passionate about marriage and family, an elder in the married council who seeks to mentor, educate and prepare the young for marriage and offer counsel to those that need it. Her heart beats for a stable family and she is a living testament to that effect. Rev. Judy is married to the man of God, AP. Ken Baraza, the overseer and vision bearer of NHOG Ministries and they are blessed with 3 beautiful daughters and thousands of spiritual children around the globe.
